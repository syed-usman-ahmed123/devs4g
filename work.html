HOW IT WORKS

stuff4GREEKS has revolutionized the way Greek paraphernalia is ordered and created. We are the only website that allows you to truly personalize every detail of your embroidered apparel - including the artwork - with no minimums. And best of all, we show you exactly how your order is going to look before we make it.

The flowchart below explains the typical process and timetable for ordering a custom product from stuff4GREEKS. This scenario reflects a crossing jacket ordered with normal turnaround time, but rush options are available.

STEP 1

TELL US WHAT YOU WANT.

Our intuitive forms allow you to easily customize your Greek apparel online. Or you can print out our fax/mail order form and send that to us. This only takes about 5 minutes once you've decided what you want to get.

STEP 2

BUY IT.

Shop securely using any major credit or debit card, or your PayPal account, or Google Checkout.

STEP 3

PREVIEW AND APPROVE.

f you ordered a custom product, then we will email you a full-color proof about two business days after we have received your order and payment. This allows you to see exactly what your order will look like before we make it. You will need to review this proof and reply to the email. You must either request changes or approve.

If you request changes, we will email you a new proof that includes your requested changes

Once we have your approval, we will begin creating your order and the final product will look exactly like your proof.

The average estimated production time for a jacket like this is 15-30 business days. If you need it guaranteed by a certain date, you can pay for expedited production.
